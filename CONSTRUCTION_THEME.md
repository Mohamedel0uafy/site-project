# Construction Industry Theme — BCGC S.A.R.L

## Design Philosophy

The website has been redesigned to reflect the construction and civil engineering industry with:

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Construction Orange (#ff6b00) — represents safety, visibility, and construction
- **Background**: Dark Industrial (#0f1419, #1a1f26) — professional, modern
- **Accents**: Bright Orange gradients for CTAs and highlights

### Visual Elements

#### 1. Blueprint Grid Pattern
- Subtle orange grid lines throughout sections
- Mimics architectural blueprints
- Adds technical/engineering feel

#### 2. Construction Stripes
- Warning stripe patterns (45° diagonal)
- Used as accent elements
- Represents construction site safety markings

#### 3. Concrete Texture
- Subtle noise overlay on body
- Gives industrial, material feel
- Non-intrusive background texture

#### 4. Industrial Typography
- Sharp, angular borders (border-radius reduced)
- Uppercase labels with wide letter-spacing
- Strong, bold headlines

#### 5. Metal/Steel Aesthetics
- Darker backgrounds with metallic gradients
- Sharp edges instead of rounded corners
- Industrial color palette
